Solfeggio frequencies

Solfeggio Frequencies are a set of frequencies, also known as ancient or healing frequencies. These frequencies have a long history and have gained much attention in certain communities over the years. Many people claim that they can impact health and well-being through sound therapy. They are most commonly played through instruments such as tuning forks and singing bowls. Source
